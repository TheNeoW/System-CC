About

System-CC is to provide a comprehensive solution for managing contracts and facilitating communication between the Divisional Secretary's Office and contractors responsible for infrastructure development projects such as building construction and road development. This system aims to streamline the contract management process, ensure adherence to rules and regulations, optimize time management, address issues related to check issuance, and enhance overall efficiency in project execution
