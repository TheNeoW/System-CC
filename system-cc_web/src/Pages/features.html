Features

  • Allow the Divisional Secretary and Assistant Director to add contracts to the system.
  • Enable contractors to request contracts from the contract queue.
  • Provide the Evaluation Committee with the ability to accept contract requests.
  • Allow contractors to upload bills for completed work
  • Enable the Divisional Secretary, Assistant Director, and Accountant to view and verify uploaded bills.
  • Facilitate communication between stakeholders through notifications.
  • Support the transfer of funds from the Divisional Secretary to contractors' bank accounts.
  • Implement holding and releasing 10% of contract funds for verification purposes
